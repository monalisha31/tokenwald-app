Getting started

Welcome to Tokenwald

Tokenwald is compliance-aware gold tokenization-as-a-service on the XRP Ledger. This guide takes a first-time user from "what is this?" to running the full lifecycle on the live XRPL Testnet — no prior blockchain knowledge assumed.

A trusted gold dealer uses Tokenwald to issue redeemable, vault-backed gold tokens (1 token = 1 gram) to KYC-verified buyers — with real-time proof-of-reserve, built-in compliance, and cross-jurisdiction recognition — without touching blockchain plumbing, and without Tokenwald ever holding the gold, the keys, or the money.

💡 The one-line mental model: the off-ledger agent proposes, a human approves, the ledger enforces, and the reserve verifies. The AI never enforces; the ledger never guesses.

What Tokenwald is (and isn't)

Tokenwald is non-custodial software. The dealer is the principal and keeps sole control of the issuer key. Tokenwald provides the rails, compliance, and trust layer underneath — think "Shopify for tokenized commodities."

Tokenwald doesTokenwald never does
Issue redeemable, 1:1 vault-backed gold tokensCustody the gold, hold keys, or route funds
Enforce who can hold tokens, on-ledgerMatch buyers to sellers / run an exchange
Publish real-time proof-of-reserveOffer yield, lending, or fractional ownership
Automate compliance paperwork & cross-border alertsBecome a financial institution

That discipline is deliberate: redeemable commodity tokens only. It's what keeps the dealer under the MiCA Article 16(2) €5M exemption and keeps Tokenwald as software, not a regulated intermediary.

The four-layer engine

Every feature in the product maps to one of four trust layers. You'll see them colour-coded throughout the app.

1 · Agentic AI — proposes. Regulation-aware automation. Reads the rules, drafts compliance, surfaces cross-jurisdiction requirements. Never enforces.

2 · Permissioned Domain — enforces. Deterministic, on-ledger gating of who may hold or receive the token. The muscle.

3 · Live Token (dynamic NFT) — records. A living, versioned compliance-state and cross-jurisdiction audit trail that travels with the asset. Informs; doesn't enforce.

4 · Proof of Reserve — verifies. Real-time, on-chain verification that the vaulted gold backs the tokens 1:1, built on XRPL's native Price Oracle. The credibility layer.

Quick start (5 minutes)

You need Node.js 18+. From the project folder:

npm install
npm start

Then open http://localhost:4600. That's it — the app creates and funds fresh XRPL Testnet wallets on demand, so there's nothing else to configure.

ℹ️ The Jurisdiction Copilot chat and the Advisor plan use Claude Sonnet 5 and need an ANTHROPIC_API_KEY in a .env file (see the project README). Everything else works without one.
⚠️ Whenever you change anything in server/, restart the server (stop it, then npm start). Frontend-only changes in public/ just need a browser refresh. A stale server is the #1 cause of "Unexpected token '<'" errors — the page received an HTML error instead of JSON from a route the old process didn't have yet.
Using the product

Guided tour

The product is one flow, end to end: Plan → Configure → Simulate → Monitor. Start at the landing page, then follow the guided flow bar, or jump straight to any view.

  1. Plan with the Tokenization Advisor — how much should the dealer tokenize?
  2. Configure with the Jurisdiction Copilot — brainstorm and set the compliance rules for your markets.
  3. Simulate in the Sandbox — run the full lifecycle on the live XRPL Testnet.
  4. Monitor in the Dashboards — track holdings and arm live-token automation rules.

1 · Tokenization Advisor

Tells the dealer how much to tokenize and how to phase it, based on how they operate today. Enter your vault gold, monthly sales, KYC-ready share, target jurisdictions, and risk appetite.

It returns a recommended initial issuance and tranche schedule (kept under the €5M MiCA cap), required reserve coverage, a projected SaaS tier, a P10/P50/P90 adoption projection, and risk flags. Click "Model this plan with Claude" for an LLM-generated plan; without an API key it falls back to a live in-browser estimate. Generate compliance-ready plan hands the numbers to the Copilot and pre-seeds the Sandbox.

2 · Jurisdiction Copilot

Two ways to use it, depending on how much you already know:

Brainstorm a jurisdiction (start here if unsure)

Pick a country and chat with the Copilot about that jurisdiction's tokenization laws — regulators, licensing triggers, KYC/AML, custody, cross-border recognition. It's grounded in Tokenwald's model and reminds you it's research, not legal advice. Powered by Claude Sonnet 5.

Analyze a regulation

Paste (or upload) regulation text and hit Analyze & draft compliance profile. The Copilot returns a structured profile — token classification, holder eligibility, KYC requirements, transfer/redemption restrictions, required filings, cross-border steps, and risk flags — then shows a diff of how your Live-Token state and domain rules would change. Apply writes it into the shared config the Sandbox and Dashboards read.

💡 The Copilot only proposes. Nothing is enforced until you apply it, and enforcement always happens through the permissioned domain and the Live Token — never by the AI.

3 · Sandbox (live Testnet)

The star of the demo: step through the full token lifecycle. With the backend running, the badge reads LIVE · XRPL TESTNET and every step submits real transactions you can verify on a block explorer. Run the steps in order:

StepWhat happensPrimitive
aOnboard the dealer, fund Issuer + two buyer walletsFaucet
bConnect the vault feed; a mint gets blocked for insufficient reserve, then unlocked after top-upPrice Oracle (XLS-47)
cCreate the permissioned domain; KYC-verify BuyerEU and issue a gold-kyc credentialXLS-80 + Credentials
dMint the gold token, capped at the attested reserveMPT
eMint the Live Token (compliance dNFT); its JSON appears on the rightDynamic NFT (XLS-46)
fCompliance gate: transfer to the uncredentialed buyer fails (tecNO_AUTH); to the credentialed buyer succeedsMPT + domain gate
gPrice-triggered auto-redeem: tokens returned, settlement paid (reads a rule you armed in Dashboards)MPT + XRP settlement
hCross-jurisdiction to Dubai: VARA/DORA alerts, recognition filing, then the resale restriction proven on-ledgerCredentials + domain

The right-hand rail shows the versioned Live-Token compliance state and a scrolling ledger. Each ledger entry links to testnet.xrpl.org. Use Restart run for a fresh set of wallets.

ℹ️ A full run takes a few minutes because every transaction waits for real Testnet ledger validation. That latency is real chain time — worth pacing for on stage.

4 · Dashboards & automation

Two personas sharing one live market feed. Toggle between them.

Issuer sees grams tokenized, reserve coverage, holders by jurisdiction, MiCA cap utilization, and fees — plus charts for supply, coverage, and projected vs. actual uptake.

Buyer/investor sees holdings, value, P/L, and jurisdiction status — and the headline feature: Live-Token automation rules. Connect your token to rules like "auto-redeem if gold drops below €X/g" (stop-loss), "auto-redeem above €Y/g" (take-profit), or "alert only." Arming a rule is your one-time human approval; the agent may then execute only that, on-ledger. Rules you arm here are read by Sandbox step g.

💡 On XRPL Testnet there's no RLUSD, so settlement in the demo is paid in XRP. In production the cash leg settles in RLUSD, atomically via Batch.

Marketplace (buyer view)

A gated storefront: only gold-kyc-credentialed wallets may buy. It's a primary market only — the dealer selling their own redeemable tokens — with no order-matching or secondary exchange. Buyers self-custody what they buy and can redeem for physical gold or cash out.
