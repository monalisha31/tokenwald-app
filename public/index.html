The problem

Why digital gold hasn't worked

01
Closed-cage "digital gold"
App-based gold can't leave the app. No self-custody, no portability, no on-chain proof — just a claim against a platform.
02
Too hard for a real dealer
A bullion dealer who wants to do it properly faces token engineering, KYC gating, MiCA filings, and oracle infrastructure. Nobody ships that alone.
03
No proof, no borders
No live proof-of-reserve, and a token that's compliant in Frankfurt is a legal question mark in Dubai. Trust stops at the border.

How it works

From vault to verified token in three steps

01
Connect your vault
Your custodian's bar-list attestation feeds an on-ledger reserve oracle. Every token is provably backed before it exists — minting is blocked the moment coverage falls short.
02
Configure compliance
Pick your jurisdictions; the compliance copilot drafts the profile and paperwork, you approve, and eligibility becomes an on-ledger rule — enforced by the ledger, not by policy documents.
03
Issue & monitor
Mint redeemable metal tokens to verified buyers, watch reserve coverage and holders in real time, and let the live compliance record travel with the asset across borders.
